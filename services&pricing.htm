10 Types of Websites and How to Choose the Right One for you

So,below are the 10 types of websites online and how to choose the right one for your business.

  1. eCommerce Website

    2. An eCommerce website allows you sell products or services on your website.An eCommerce website is essentially an online store.Whether you're selling your own products or services or using a dropshipping model, they both fall under eCommerce websites.

  2. Business Website

    3. A business website establishes your brand's online presence.This website does not offer products or services that can be purchased directly from the website.Instead,the website explains to customers what the business does and offers contact information or a booking system for a consulation.

  3. Blog Website

    4. Blogs are one of the most popular types of websites online and are great for people who want to share written and visual content about whatever they want.

    Some people turn their blogs into businesses by working with advertiser or affiliates.

  4. Portfolio Website

    5. A portfolio website is a place where certain professionals can display samples of thier work.Then,prospective employers can browse through their portfolio website and assess their skills and knowledge.

  5. Event Website

    6. An event website is a website where you can market the events you're organizing.An event website can also be where you hold virtual events as well

  6. Personal Website

    7. A personal website can be used to promote yourself or an interest of yours.For example, a personal website can be a CV or RESUME website where you present who you are and your professional accomplishment.

    This type of website is not a neccesity but is a great way to boost your online presence and bring eyes to you.

  7. Membership Website

    8. A membership website gives exclusive access to vistors who sign up or pay a fee to access the website's full services

  8. Nonprofit Website

    9. A nonprofit website provides information to visitors about your organization's mission,values,and goals.Nonprofit websites include information about latest events and donations page.

  9. Informational Website

    10. informational websites are a resource center for information on multiple or simple topics.

    These website includes long-form articles majorly for search function

  10. Online Forum

    11. An online forum is a space where you can build a community around a specific interest.

    In online forum, users can engage in debate, discuss topics and interact with other users and can be monetized by including membership access.
